Meet Cat Town’s new Executive Director, Andrew Dorman! We are so excited to have Andrew in this role. Andrew has more than a decade of experience managing teams and multi-million dollar projects, in addition to his years of experience developing and implementing solutions to support animal welfare in Oakland on the Friends of Oakland Animal Services Board of Directors. With his dedication to Cat Town and to our friends at @oaklandanimalservices, he’s the perfect person to continue Ann's legacy. Read about how he first became a “cat person,” why he loves Cat Town, and his plans for the future on our blog: bit.ly/cattownandrewdorman
